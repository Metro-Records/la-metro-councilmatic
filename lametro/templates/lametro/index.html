The Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority, commonly known as Metro, consists of several Board Members, who set policy, coordinate, plan, fund, build, and operate transit services and transportation programs throughout Los Angeles County.

Metro Board tracks all things related to the Metro Board of Directors:

  • the legislation introduced and passed
  • its various committees and the meetings they hold
  • the board members themselves
